## 摘要
我们研究了度量图上的薛定谔问题及其不同表述。从静态版本出发，我们引入了一个等价的熵最优输运表述，并展示了其向静态最优输运的Γ-收敛性。然后，我们严格推导了薛定谔问题的Benamou-Brenier型动态版本，从而将已知结果推广到RCD*(K,N)-空间。利用这一等价性，我们得出动态薛定谔问题的最小值收敛到平方Wasserstein距离，并且极小化子收敛到Wasserstein测地线。我们还将动态表述推广到更一般的初末数据类，并通过直接方法证明了该设定下解的存在性。最后，我们通过数值研究说明了我们的分析结果。

## Abstract
We study the Schrödinger problem on metric graphs and its different formulations. Starting from a static version, we introduce an equivalent reformulation as entropic optimal transport and show $Γ$-convergence towards static optimal transport. We then rigorously derive a Benamou-Brenier type dynamic version of the Schrödinger problem, thereby extending known results from ${\rm RCD}^*(K,N)$-spaces. With this equivalence at hand, we conclude that the minimum values of the dynamic Schrödinger problem converge towards the squared Wasserstein distance, and minimizers converge to Wasserstein geodesics. We also extend the dynamic formulation to a more general class of initial and final data and show existence of solutions in this setting using the direct method. Lastly, we illustrate our analytical findings by a numerical investigation.
