## 摘要
$q$-Bass鞅类为鞅最优传输中的一个核心问题提供了自然答案：如何构造具有给定初始和终端边际分布且转移核尽可能接近给定参考测度$q$的鞅。我们证明了当初始边际分布支撑在有限多个原子上时，$q$-Bass鞅的存在性，并建立了相关Bass测度的唯一性（相差一个加法平移常数）。我们的方法是几何的，依赖于对凸多边形链的适当参数化的分析。

## Abstract
The class of $q$-Bass martingales provides a natural answer to a central question in martingale optimal transport: how to construct martingales with prescribed initial and terminal marginals whose transition kernel remains as close as possible to a given reference measure $q$. We prove the existence of $q$-Bass martingales when the initial marginal is supported on finitely many atoms, and establish uniqueness, up to an additive translation constant, of the associated Bass measure. Our approach is geometric and relies on the analysis of a suitable parametrization of convex polygonal chains.
