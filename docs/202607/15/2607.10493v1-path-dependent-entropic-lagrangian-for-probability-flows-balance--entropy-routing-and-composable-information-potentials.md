## 摘要
概率分布是信息论、统计推断和现代概率学习的核心。最大熵在指定约束下选择概率状态，但未指定如何达到该状态、概率如何传输，以及沿着路径如何考虑耗散和外部信息交换。我们发展了一种路径依赖的熵拉格朗日微积分，通过受限生成元、上限历史项和显式平衡-熵端口路由，将静态状态选择扩展到概率路径演化。该构造在标准迁移率闭包下产生热状态关系、保守概率平衡和非负产生。其KL/香农部分将最大熵和贝叶斯定律恢复为静态无通量状态，而时间相关信息势则将内部耗散与供给信息功率分离。可组合信息和结构势控制尾部、稀疏性、鲁棒性、正则化和非局部多模态，而不改变核算架构。两个数值例子验证了质量守恒、能量分解和总自由能分类账。

## Abstract
Probability distributions are central to information theory, statistical inference, and modern probabilistic learning. Maximum entropy selects a probability state under prescribed constraints, but it does not specify how that state is reached, how probability is transported, or how dissipation and external information exchange are accounted for along the path. We develop a path-dependent entropic Lagrangian calculus that extends static state selection to probability-path evolution through restricted generators, upper-limit history terms, and explicit balance--entropy port routing. The construction yields the thermal state relation, conservative probability balance, and nonnegative production under standard mobility closure. Its KL/Shannon sector recovers maximum-entropy and Bayesian laws as stationary no-flux states, while time-dependent information potentials separate internal dissipation from supplied information power. Composable information and structural potentials control tails, sparsity, robustness, regularization, and nonlocal multimodality without changing the accounting architecture. Two numerical examples verify mass conservation, energy decomposition, and the total free-energy ledger.
