## Abstract
Diffusion models have emerged as powerful tools for generative modeling, demonstrating exceptional capability in capturing target data distributions from large datasets. However, fine-tuning these massive models for specific downstream tasks, constraints, and human preferences remains a critical challenge. While recent advances have leveraged reinforcement learning algorithms to tackle this problem, much of the progress has been empirical, with limited theoretical understanding. To bridge this gap, we propose a stochastic control framework for fine-tuning diffusion models. Building on denoising diffusion probabilistic models as the pre-trained reference dynamics, our approach integrates linear dynamics control with Kullback–Leibler regularization. We establish the well-posedness and regularity of the stochastic control problem and develop a {policy iteration algorithm (PI-FT)} for numerical solution. We show that PI-FT achieves global convergence at a linear rate. Unlike existing work that assumes regularities throughout training, we prove that the control and value sequences generated by the algorithm preserve the desired regularity. Finally, we extend our framework to parametric settings for efficient implementation and demonstrate the practical effectiveness of the proposed PI-FT algorithm through numerical experiments.

## 2. 方法论
### 2.1 随机控制框架
- **模型构建**：将预训练 DDPM 的逆向扩散过程视为参考动力学：
  \[
  Y_{t+1}^{\text{pre}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\alpha_t}}(Y_t^{\text{pre}} + (1-\alpha_t)s_t^{\text{pre}}(Y_t^{\text{pre}})) + \sigma_t W_t.
  \]
- **微调目标**：引入控制策略 \(u_t(Y_t)\) 替代预训练分数函数 \(s_t^{\text{pre}}\)，并最大化带 KL 正则化的期望奖励：
  \[
  \mathbb{E}\left[ R(Y_T) - \sum_{t=0}^{T-1} \beta_t \cdot \text{KL}(p_{t+1|t}(\cdot|Y_t) \| p_{t+1|t}^{\text{pre}}(\cdot|Y_t)) \right].
  \]
- **关键简化**：KL 散度在动态为高斯时等价于平方误差：
  \[
  \text{KL} = \frac{(1-\alpha_t)^2}{2\alpha_t\sigma_t^2} \|u_t(y) - s_t^{\text{pre}}(y)\|_2^2.
  \]
  因此，微调问题转化为带平方惩罚的随机最优控制问题，其中控制量直接代表微调后的分数函数。

### 2.2 理论性质（正则性与良好定性）
- **假设**：期望奖励 \(r(y)=\mathbb{E}[R(y)]\) 和预训练分数 \(s_t^{\text{pre}}\) 均满足 Lipschitz 光滑性。
- **核心结果**（定理 2.8）：当 KL 正则化系数 \(\beta_t\) 充分大时：
  - 最优值函数 \(V_t^*(y)\) 是 Lipschitz 且梯度 Lipschitz 的。
  - 存在唯一最优控制 \(u_t^*(y)\)，并满足隐式方程：
    \[
    u_t^*(y) = s_t^{\text{pre}}(y) + \frac{\sqrt{\alpha_t}\sigma_t^2}{(1-\alpha_t)\beta_t} \mathbb{E}[\nabla V_{t+1}^*(y')].
    \]
  - 最优控制同样具备 Lipschitz 和梯度 Lipschitz 性质。
- **证明技巧**：利用动态规划原理、KL 项带来的单步强凹性、高斯平滑效应以及分部积分公式，通过反向归纳完成。

### 2.3 算法设计：PI-FT（Policy Iteration for Fine-Tuning）
- **思路**：基于最优控制满足的不动点方程，设计迭代策略：
  1. 初始化：\(u_t^{(0)} = s_t^{\text{pre}}\).
  2. 第 \(m+1\) 次更新：
     \[
     u_t^{(m+1)}(y) = s_t^{\text{pre}}(y) + \frac{\sqrt{\alpha_t}\sigma_t^2}{(1-\alpha_t)\beta_t} \mathbb{E}\left[ \nabla V_{t+1}^{(m_{t+1})}( \frac{1}{\sqrt{\alpha_t}}(y + (1-\alpha_t)u_t^{(m)}(y)) + \sigma_t W_t ) \right].
     \]
  3. 同步计算值函数 \(V_t^{(m_t)}\)。
- **收敛性**（定理 3.1）：
  - 算法生成的迭代序列具有一致的正则性上界（不依赖迭代次数）。
  - 控制序列和值函数梯度均以线性速率收敛至最优：
    \[
    \|u_t^{(m)} - u_t^*\|_2 = O((1-\lambda)^m),\quad \| \nabla V_t^{(m)} - \nabla V_t^* \|_2 = O((1-\lambda)^{m+1} ).
    \]
  - 其中 \(\lambda \in (0,1)\) 由 \(\beta_t\) 决定，\(\beta_t\) 越大收敛越快但模型越接近预训练模型。

### 2.4 参数化扩展
- 将控制表示为线性基函数组合：\(u_t(y) = K_t \phi(y)\)。
- 证明在此参数化下，若最优控制可实现，则目标函数有唯一平稳点，且策略梯度方法可达到线性收敛。
