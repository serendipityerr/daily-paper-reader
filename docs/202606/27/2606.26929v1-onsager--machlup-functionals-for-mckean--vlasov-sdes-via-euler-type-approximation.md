## 摘要
Onsager-Machlup作用泛函为刻画随机系统最可能跃迁路径提供了变分框架，在非平衡涨落研究中具有重要作用。将其推广到McKean-Vlasov随机微分方程时，由于系数的内在分布依赖性而变得复杂。本文通过引入基于经典非分布依赖随机微分方程的欧拉型近似方案来解决这一困难。结合经典Onsager-Machlup理论与近似序列的收敛性论证，我们推导出与McKean-Vlasov随机微分方程相关的Onsager-Machlup泛函的显式表达式。所提方法具有构造性，并可推广到一大类分布依赖随机系统。

## Abstract
The Onsager--Machlup action functional provides a variational framework for characterizing the most probable transition paths of stochastic systems and plays an important role in the study of nonequilibrium fluctuations. Its extension to McKean--Vlasov stochastic differential equations is complicated by the intrinsic distribution dependence of the coefficients. In this paper, we address this difficulty by introducing an Euler-type approximation scheme based on classical, distribution-free stochastic differential equations. Combining the classical Onsager--Machlup theory with a convergence argument for the approximation sequence, we derive an explicit expression for the Onsager--Machlup functional associated with the McKean--Vlasov SDE. The proposed approach is constructive and extends to a broad class of distribution-dependent stochastic systems.
