## 摘要
研究了一个半鞅最优运输（SOT）问题，其中成本依赖于完整的微分特征，且最小化是在所有给定边际分布且绝对连续特征位于规定闭凸集内的半鞅律上进行的。在成本函数仅满足可测性、凸性和下半连续性的最小假设下，我们证明了SOT最优计划的存在性，并建立了没有时间正则性条件的Kantorovich型对偶性。进一步证明了SOT有四种等价表述：(i) Kantorovich对偶表述，(ii) Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman方程的粘性解表述，(iii) 通过马尔可夫投影的鞅解表述，(iv) 通过非局部分布-柯尔莫哥洛夫方程弱解的偏微分方程表述。该框架同时推广了经典最优运输、鞅最优运输、薛定谔桥问题和重心弱最优运输。

## Abstract
We study a semimartingale optimal transport (SOT) problem where the cost depends on the full differential characteristics, and the minimisation is over all semimartingale laws with given marginals whose absolutely continuous characteristics lie in a prescribed closed convex set. Under only minimal assumptions of measurability, convexity, and lower semicontinuity on the cost function, we prove the existence of an optimal plan for SOT and establish a Kantorovich-type duality without time-regularity conditions. We further prove that SOT admits four equivalent formulations: (i) a Kantorovich duality formulation, (ii) a viscosity solution formulation of the Hamilton--Jacobi--Bellman equation, (iii) a martingale solution formulation via Markovian projection, (iv) a PDE formulation via weak solutions of a non-local Fokker--Planck--Kolmogorov equation. The framework simultaneously generalises classical optimal transport, martingale optimal transport, Schrödinger bridge problems, and barycentric weak optimal transport.
